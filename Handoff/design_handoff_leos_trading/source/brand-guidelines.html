"A UAE-based global supplier of OEM parts, industrial equipment and sourcing solutions."

Premium industrial positioning — closer to Caterpillar, Komatsu and Bosch Industrial than to automotive retail or e-commerce. Confident, technical, spec-sheet tone throughout.

Photography

Enhancement Guide

Uploaded assets are real company material, evaluated below for composition, lighting, sharpness, background and branding, with a recommendation for use. A second upload batch (warehouse shots, inventory spreadsheets, additional product photos) confirmed real inventory depth: 2,256 SKUs across Kohler (329), Iveco (1,614) and Kobelco (313), ~20,000 units on hand. Several new images were manufacturer marketing graphics or generic stock photography (Kobelco/Iveco brochure art, a tire lineup, a boat-parts diagram, a "Parts of Generator" infographic) — excluded from the public site for the same reason as the brochure page below: not Leos-owned photography, some showing third-party branding. The usable additions (Kohler XTX engine, a diesel filter cartridge, a PCB control board, a bronze sea-water pump) are used as category-level imagery only, never pinned to a specific SKU record unless the photo carries no conflicting printed part number.

Command PRO 20 Engine

Use as-is
Composition

Clean 3/4 hero angle, good subject prominence.

Lighting

Alternator + Kohler Box

Enhance before use
Composition

Decent overhead symmetry, but domestic wood-floor setting undercuts premium-industrial feel.

Two Pexels Warehouse Photos

Excluded
Composition

Wide racking shots — visually strong, well lit and sharp.

Branding

They depict a home-appliance / refrigerator warehouse — visible LG, Zephyr and Liebherr branding on cartons — not Leos' actual generator-parts operation.

Motion

Motion Language

Restrained and functional — motion confirms an action, it never decorates.

  • 150–200ms ease transitions on hover/focus states — buttons, links, cards
  • No parallax, no auto-playing carousels, no decorative motion on load
  • RFQ/search state changes (filtering, submit confirmation) respond instantly, no artificial delay
Scalability

Future Scalability Plan

The architecture is built for thousands of part numbers across unlimited brands and categories from day one.

01

Category → Brand → Part

Fixed three-level taxonomy. New brands slot under existing categories without new page templates.

02

03

04
